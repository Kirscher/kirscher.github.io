title: Awarded the CNRS GdR IASIS Doctoral Mobility Grant
---

I have been awarded the **CNRS GdR IASIS Doctoral Mobility Grant** (2026), a competitive national funding program supporting doctoral research stays in France and abroad.

This grant supports my visiting research stay at the [Medical Image Computing (MIC)](https://www.dkfz.de/en/mic/index.php) team at the [German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ)](https://www.dkfz.de/en/index.html) in Heidelberg, Germany, where I am working on uncertainty-aware deep learning for radiotherapy segmentation.

The [GdR IASIS](https://gdr-iasis.cnrs.fr/) (Groupement de Recherche en Information, Signal, Image et viSion) is a CNRS research network bringing together the French research community in signal processing, image analysis, and computer vision.
