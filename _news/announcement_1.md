title: Started PhD at the University of Strasbourg

I have started my **PhD in Applied Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence** at the [University of Strasbourg](https://www.unistra.fr/), jointly affiliated with [ICube IMAGeS](https://images.icube.unistra.fr/en/index.php/Home) and [Institut Strauss](https://www.institut-strauss.fr/).

My research focuses on uncertainty quantification for deep learning-based segmentation in radiotherapy, with emphasis on calibration, robustness, and clinical decision support. I am supervised by [S. Faisan](https://images.icube.unistra.fr/en/index.php/Sylvain_Faisan) (PhD, HDR), P. Meyer (PhD, HDR), and X. Coubez (PhD).

This PhD is funded by the **European ITI HealthTech PhD Scholarship**, a competitive program supported by the University of Strasbourg in partnership with CNRS and Inserm under the Excellence Initiative (IdEx) program.
